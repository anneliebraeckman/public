INFO

I'm Annelie Braeckman and I'm an artist from Belgium. I am currently in the third year of my studies.
I study visual arts at the Luca School of Arts in Ghent. My focus for my work is mainly on drawing and painting.
I mainly get my inspiration from my life now or my memories. Many moments or objects from my childhood provide a starting point for work.
